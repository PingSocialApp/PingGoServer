Information we collect:

Our app needs your input of basic contact information in order to create your accounts, such as your name and email address. In order to find events near you, we ask for your permission to use your location while the app is in use.  Your location will be used to find events and promotions near you.

Optional information you provide:

Ping provides a QR scanning feature that allows you to share some information about yourself with connections. In this feature, you control the ability to share your social media usernames, relevant URLs, and email/phone number. This is completely optional for you to use; users are given the option to provide their usernames for Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, Snapchat, TikTok, and Venmo. Users are additionally given the option to provide URLs for a website and LinkedIn and are given the option to provide email addresses and a phone number. This data is not to be sold, distributed, or shared in any manner other than what each individual user allows other users to see.

Do we use cookies or other tracking technologies?

Ping does not use or collect cookies or other similar tracking technologies.

How we use your information

Ping uses your name and email address to create your account. We need access to your location data in order to use our main feature: the map. No other data is needed for use of the app, however we have provided the ability for you to share your contact or professional information to people you meet! This optional information is just stored by us - we will not sell or distribute your provided contact information to third parties.

Will my information be shared?

By using Ping, the name you provide will be shared with event hosts and other users. The rest of the information we collect will only be shared at your discretion (e.g., social media usernames, website URLs, contact information).

Ping will not disclose, distribute, or sell personal information you provide us to third party organizations, unless there are explicit circumstances in which disclosure is required by law.

How long will my data be stored?

Your data will be stored as long as it is needed for legitimate business purposes and is permitted by law. We will delete your data if you decide to terminate your account - unless we must keep it for an outstanding legal issue, claim, dispute, or fraud protection.

Data rights

You have the right to request to see your data, make changes to your data, and delete your data no matter where you reside - not just in California!

How can I review or make changes to the data you collect about me?

Contact Ping using our information below and you will receive more information on the process of doing so.

Can I delete my data or account?

You can! Unless there is an ongoing legal case, you are able to delete your account and information from our records.

Changes to this privacy policy

This privacy policy was last revised on 09/09/2021. We maintain full discretion to change this policy at any time and for any reason. We encourage you to review this privacy policy each time you visit the website.

If we make a significant change to our privacy policy, we will notify users on the homepage of our website or application for a period of time after the change is made.

Responsibility for external sites:

While using Ping, you may encounter links to other websites. Some of those websites may be operated by third parties. We provide the links for your convenience, but we do not review, control, or monitor the privacy practices of websites operated by others.

We are not responsible for any data collected on these external websites operated by third parties.  Whenever you leave the Ping website or app, it is recommended that you review their privacy policy to be aware of what they are doing with your data.
